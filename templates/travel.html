Getting There

The Anacortes ferry terminal is a 2-hour drive north from Seatac airport, then it's an extremely pretty 45-minute ferry ride from Anacortes to Orcas Island. Once you're on the island, the farm and town are both only a few miles from the ferry terminal.

NOTE: The ferry is prone to running late and occasionally gets canceled. We strongly encourage you to get to the island on Friday so you don't miss the wedding on Saturday. We will have a bunch of fun things planned to make it worth your while! We'll go whale watching and, in the evening, we'll have dinner at Pebble Cove Farm. We hope you'll join us!

Having a car will help you make the most of your time on Orcas, but if you aren't able to get a vehicle reservation, we'll do some shuttling/carpooling on the island. There is a big parking lot at the ferry terminal in Anacortes where you'll be able to leave your car.

Where Should I Stay?

We've reserved a number of lodging options through Pebble Cove Farm, the Outlook Inn, and Deer Harbor Inn. When you RSVP, reach out to us and we can help you pick one and make sure you're accounted for!

Options

  • Pebble Cove Farm: A number of rooms on site and a few cottages and townhouses in Eastsound. We rented them all out, so please give us a call and we'll set you up with one! View accommodations
  • The Outlook Inn: Located in Eastsound, 8 miles from the farm. Reserve by calling (360) 376-2200 and request the Sulgrove/Burgoyne Wedding Room Block for a discounted rate of $220/night. Let us know if you choose this option! Visit website
  • Deer Harbor Inn: Two little cabins in a quiet spot 2 miles from the farm. Reach out to us and we'll set you up with one! Visit website
